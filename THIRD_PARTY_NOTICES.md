## ebook2cw

MorseBook is inspired by `ebook2cw`, which converts text ebooks into Morse/CW
audio files.

- Author: Fabian Kurz, DJ5CW
- Website: <https://fkurz.net/ham/ebook2cw.html>
- Source: <https://git.fkurz.net/dj1yfk/ebook2cw>
- License: GPLv2, as stated on the ebook2cw project page

MorseBook does not vendor, link, or redistribute `ebook2cw` code. It is an
independent implementation with a browser playback workflow inspired by the
general idea of converting ebook text into CW practice material.
